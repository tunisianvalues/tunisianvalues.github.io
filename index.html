WHAT IS TUNISIAN VALUES?

81 questions • 7 political axes • 10 minutes

TunisianValues is a political quiz adapted to the Tunisian context. You will answer 81 statements based on your level of agreement, from "Strongly Agree" to "Strongly Disagree."

7 POLITICAL AXES

A multidimensional analysis of your values

At the end, your answers will place you on 7 major axes reflecting different political views present in Tunisia: Pan-Arabism, International Cooperation, Economy, Religion, Society, Democracy, and Governance.

Each axis represents a spectrum of political opinions present in the Tunisian landscape.
